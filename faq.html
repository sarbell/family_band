Faq

Whether it is a wedding, or a hoedown at the park, we've got you covered.

We play a variety of genres from Bluegrass to Oldies, Sacred to Patriotic, and Rock n’ Roll to Funk. Playing at a wide variety of venues allows us to fulfill our mission of sharing faith and family throughout Northern Utah.
