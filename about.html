About

Core Values

The Cox and Palkki families both have a multi-generational heritage of faith and music, which they celebrate and strive to cultivate.

History

The Cox and Palkki families met in 2012 while participating in a summer music camp. After their initial connection, they found they had much in common, including their passion and love for creating and sharing music.

In the fall of 2013, the ______ Band was created. Since that time, they have enjoyed performing at countless venues for various audiences throughout Northern Utah. They feel a deep sense of purpose and joy as they celebrate their heritage of faith, family, and music together.

Meet the Band

The band members from the Cox and Palkki families range from ages 12 to 75. Each member plays at least one instrument and they also enjoy singing together.
