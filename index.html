What We Do

We are a family band made up of multiple families who love to perform music. In a normal year (as compared to this year with the pandemic) we would hold a busy schedule of performances. Our main performances are at resthomes, the veterans home in Ogden, church parties, charity fundraisers, dances, and community gatherings. We are constantly expanding our repertoire to include everything from classic jazz standards, to funky rock songs to make sure we have exactly what you need at your event.

Why do we do this? Because our families are close friends and music has brought us even closer. If we can help other people draw closer together and take a break from this crazy world, we want to do that! We strive to make the world a better place through music.
